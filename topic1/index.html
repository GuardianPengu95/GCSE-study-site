What you need to be able to do (Grade 9 focus)

  • Compare animal, plant, and prokaryotic cells using correct terminology.
  • Use microscopy terms correctly and calculate magnification.
  • Explain diffusion, osmosis, and active transport using particle reasoning.
  • Explain mitosis and why it matters for growth and repair.
  • Evaluate stem cell uses with benefits, risks, and ethics.
